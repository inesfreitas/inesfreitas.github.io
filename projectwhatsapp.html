whatsapp: new feature.

the challenge.

The main challenge was the creation of a new feature for a widely know app. This feature needed to be something new that would add value for the users of this app.

To handle his challenge we decided to develop a new feature for WhatsApp.

why whatsapp?

WhatsApp is the most popular mobile messaging app worldwide.

WhatsApp's impact on the mobile phone industry and internet access in developing countries.

WhatsApp's encryption and privacy features and their impact on user security.

analysis and discovery.

Did you know that an average user opens WhatsApp 23-25 times a day?

But such a number doesn’t come as a surprise because users on an average spend 38 minutes using the app daily, which comes to about 19.4 hours a month. WhatsApp voice calls are also gaining steady popularity.

On an average day, users spend a total of 2 billion minutes on WhatsApp voice and video calls.

survey analysis and benchmark.

Following the analysis phase, we created a survey for Whatsapp users in order to find out what where the main problems they felt and how they use the app on a daily basis.

We also asked what other apps do they use, if they prefered them over Whatsapp and why.
